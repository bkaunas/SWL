Overview

Over the years my dad and I have done a number of 3-D printing projects on our old cheap printer. Lately the process flow has been my dad asking me to make something for him, I CAD it up and print it. A very one sided relationship if you ask me. HA. Some of the projects include making headset stands, replacement parts, and other miscellaneous small convenience items. I also did some CAD work for the array layout on the solar car team!
